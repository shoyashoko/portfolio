A Silent Voice is a 2016 japanese animated flim directed by Naoko Yamada and based on the manga by Yoshitoki Oima.

Intersing Fact's

  • The flim has a beautiful and expressive animation styke that enhances the emotional impact of the story.
    The use of colors,lighting,and camera angles creates a vivid and immersive atmosphere that draws the viewer into the characters world.
  • The flim has a realistic and diverse cast of character that are well developed and relatable.
    Each character has theri own flaws,struggles,and growth, and they interact with each other in complex and meaningful ways.
    The flim does not shy away from showing the dark and ugly sides of human nature, but also the potential for redemption and forgiveness.
  • The flim has a powerful and relevant message that resonates with the contemporary audience.
    the flim addresses the serious and sensitive issues of bullying, mental health, disability, and suicide, and how they affect the lives of the victims and the perpetrators.
